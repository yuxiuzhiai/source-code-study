ImportSelector

注释

    用于决定哪个@Configuration类应该被导入,基于一个给定的选择标准,通常是一或多个注解

子接口

	DeferredImportSelector:在所有@Configuration类解析完之后运行.这个类型的selector在被选中的import是@Conditional的时候非常有用

		实现类:
			AutoConfigurationImportSelector:处理@EnableAutoConfiguration
			ImportAutoConfigurationImportSelector:继承自AutoConfigurationImportSelector,处理@ImportAutoConfiguration

子类

	EnableConfigurationPropertiesImportSelector:通过@ConfigurationProperties绑定外部properties到@Configuration类
	CachingConfigurationSelector:@EnableCaching相关
	AsyncConfigurationSelector:@EnableAsync相关
